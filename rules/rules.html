Uxarieus Town Ordinances

⚠️ Violations are tracked. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. ⚠️

01

Respect the Landscape

No floating trees, no random 1x1 towers, and no creeper holes. If a creeper blows up, fix the terrain immediately.

02

Building Permits

Consult Jayden or K256 before starting a build. Unapproved structures that ruin the town aesthetic will be demolished without refund.

03

Resource Etiquette

Public farms must be replanted. Do not empty shared chests for personal hoard; take only what you need to survive.
